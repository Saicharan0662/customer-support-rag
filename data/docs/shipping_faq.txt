ACME Store Shipping FAQ

Standard Shipping
Orders ship within 1 to 2 business days from our New Jersey warehouse. Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days within the continental United States. Standard shipping is free on orders over 50 dollars. Orders under 50 dollars have a flat 6 dollar shipping fee.

Express Shipping
Express shipping arrives in 1 to 2 business days. Cost is 18 dollars regardless of order size. Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern Time ship the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM ship the next business day.

International Shipping
We ship to Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and the European Union. International orders take 7 to 14 business days. Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the customer. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Tracking
A tracking number is emailed within 24 hours of your order shipping. Track your package at acmestore.example/track or directly on the carrier website. We use UPS for domestic orders and DHL for international orders.

Lost or Delayed Packages
If your tracking has not updated for 5 business days, contact support. We will open an investigation with the carrier. Most delays resolve within 48 hours. If the package is confirmed lost, we ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund.

PO Boxes and APO Addresses
We ship to PO boxes and APO and FPO addresses through USPS only. Standard shipping times apply. Express shipping is not available for these addresses.
